About The Game

Count Masters: Stickman Games is an exciting multiplayer game where you control a group of stickman warriors. Your goal is to grow your army by collecting stickmen and defeating enemies in various challenging levels.

The game features simple yet addictive gameplay mechanics. You start with a small group of stick figures and must navigate through obstacles while collecting more teammates. Face off against enemy groups in epic battles where numbers matter!

With colorful graphics, smooth animations, and progressively challenging levels, Count Masters: Stickman Games offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages.

Army Building

Multiple Levels

Progress through various challenging stages with increasing difficulty and unique obstacles.

How To Play

  1. Control your stickman army by dragging left or right.
  2. Collect more stickmen by running over number gates.
  3. Avoid obstacles and enemy armies that are larger than yours.
  4. Defeat enemies by having a larger army when you collide.
  5. Complete all challenges to progress to the next level.

The game is easy to learn but challenging to master. Your strategy will determine how successfully you can grow and maintain your army.
